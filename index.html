/ How It Works

Five steps
to yield.

The bot screens pairs for high funding APR, opens a hedged position, maintains the delta, then exits cleanly when conditions change.

01 —
Screening

Monitors KuCoin pairs in real time. Triggers when annualized funding APR exceeds the configured threshold.

02 —
Entry

Opens a delta-neutral position: short the perpetual future, long the same asset on spot.

03 —
Delta Maintenance

Rebalances the hedge as prices drift, keeping net exposure near zero.

04 —
Exit

Closes both legs cleanly when funding drops below threshold or risk limits are hit.
